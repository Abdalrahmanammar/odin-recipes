Description

Spaghetti is a type of pasta that is long, thin, and cylindrical. It is made from wheat flour and water, and is typically boiled until it is tender. Spaghetti is often served with a variety of sauces, such as marinara, meat sauce, or Alfredo sauce. It can also be topped with cheese, herbs, and other ingredients to add flavor. Spaghetti is a popular dish around the world and is enjoyed by people of all ages.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 4 cups marinara sauce
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the spaghetti and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain the spaghetti and set aside.
  2. In a large saucepan, heat the marinara sauce over medium heat until it is warmed through.
  3. Add the cooked spaghetti to the saucepan with the marinara sauce. Toss to coat the spaghetti evenly with the sauce.
  4. Serve the spaghetti in bowls, topped with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves if desired.
