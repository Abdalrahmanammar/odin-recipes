Chocolate Cake

HOME

Description

Chocolate cake is a rich and decadent dessert that is made with cocoa powder or melted chocolate. It typically consists of layers of moist chocolate cake filled with chocolate frosting or ganache. The cake can be made with various ingredients such as flour, sugar, eggs, butter, and milk, along with the chocolate component. Chocolate cake is often enjoyed on special occasions like birthdays and celebrations, and it can be decorated with additional toppings such as sprinkles, chocolate shavings, or fresh fruit.

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, cocoa powder, sugar, and salt.
  3. Add the eggs, milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Beat on medium speed for 2 minutes until well combined.
  4. Stir in the boiling water (the batter will be thin). Pour the batter evenly into the prepared cake pans.
  5. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes, or until a tooth pick inserted into the center of the cakes comes out clean. Remove from the oven and allow the cakes to cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then turn them out onto a wire rack to cool completely.
  6. Once the cakes are completely cool, you can frost them with your favorite chocolate frosting or ganache. Spread the frosting evenly between the layers and on the top and sides of the cake.
  7. Decorate the cake as desired, and enjoy!
